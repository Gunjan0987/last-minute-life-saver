# last-minute-life-saver
LifeSaver AI is an AI-powered productivity companion that proactively discovers tasks, predicts deadline risks, intelligently prioritizes work, and helps users complete important commitments before deadlines are missed
LifeSaver AI is an AI-powered executive productivity companion designed to help students, professionals, and entrepreneurs stay ahead of their commitments and never miss important deadlines. Unlike traditional reminder applications that rely on passive notifications, LifeSaver AI proactively analyzes tasks, calendars, emails, and user priorities to intelligently plan, prioritize, and optimize daily schedules.

Powered by Google Gemini AI, the application automatically discovers important tasks, predicts deadline risks, recommends the next best action, and dynamically adjusts schedules based on changing priorities. Its unique Emergency Rescue Mode activates when critical deadlines are approaching, generating an optimized action plan to maximize the chances of successful task completion.

By combining intelligent task discovery, AI-driven scheduling, proactive decision-making, and personalized productivity insights, LifeSaver AI transforms productivity management from simple reminders into an intelligent execution system that helps users make better decisions, reduce stress, and complete their work on time.
